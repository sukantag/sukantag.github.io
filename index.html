    Jianrui Zhang, Hyun Jung Lee, Sukanta Ganguly, Tae-Eui Kam, Donghyun Kim, Yong Jae Lee
    arXiv:2605.24938 — A plug-and-play framework (SMART) that unlocks latent multi-vector capabilities in standard single-vector retrievers via late-interaction over frozen hidden states, improving multimodal retrieval across diverse modalities.
